Note: Phase 1A (material characterization) actually started in the 4th week of Jun-2026, before this chart's start date; its bar is clipped to begin at the 1st week of Jul-2026.  •  The board is locked by default. Turn on Edit mode above and enter the password to reveal checkboxes, pencil icons, and delete controls — every change still asks for confirmation.  •  In Edit mode, hover or click once on any task/sub-step (left label or the bar) to preview its Note without opening the editor. Use the ▲ / ▼ buttons to reorder task groups and sub-steps. Click the ▾ / ▸ arrow on a task group (any mode) to hide/show its sub-steps.  •  In Edit mode, drag a bar to move it or drag its right edge to resize — it snaps to individual weeks (W1–W4) within each month, and works together with the project / status filters in the toolbar. You can also set exact From/To week in the sub-step editor.  •  A thick red line marks a sub-step's Due date and a thick black line marks its Completed date, both drawn on the bar itself. In the sub-step editor, use + Add phase to break one sub-step into several dated phases (e.g. Jul W1→W4 "waiting quotation", then Aug W1→W2 "onsite check"), each with its own status and short note shown right on the bar.  •  Set status to ✓ Completed and enter a completion date — the sub-step's label also gets a "Completed" badge for quick scanning.  •  Use 🎨 Projects (Edit mode) to add, rename, recolour, or delete projects — every project/status filter, legend swatch, and bar colour updates automatically.  •  Use the － / ＋ zoom buttons (top right of the toolbar) to scale the whole board, like a browser zoom.  •  Use ⬇ Export JSON to download the full dataset (tasks + projects) as a .json file, e.g. to import into another dashboard.  •  Use the 💰 Budget button to show/hide the budget column, and click the progress ring in the header for an overall completion memo with a project and PIC breakdown.
